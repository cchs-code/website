Join

  1. Make sure you are in our discord, remind, and schoology group for updates.
  2. If you have a PC you can use, I would recommend installing vscode. Alternatively, you can sign up for Replit, which is an online platform for coding and hosting projects
  3. Join the Replit group here.

Keep in mind that being a member of the club requires that you participate and contribute. This means following your role in team projects and completing projects on your own time. Those who do not will be less likely to receive positions and service hours.
